About Pooja

Creative,
Passionate
& Charismatic

— Pooja Khurana believes in ‘Being Beautiful by being you’. Her love for the
art of beauty is beyond comparison, as she employs her extensive knowledge
and impeccable skills to craft tailor-made beauty and hair makeovers
that are inimitable. Placing quality over quantity, it has always been Pooja’s
mission to offer remarkable services that puts you at ease, while indulging in
an elite experience.

A London College of Fashion alumna, Pooja trained at the Delamar
Academy, specializing in fashion, bridal, creative, and editorial makeup
techniques. Now based in New Delhi, her passion for fashion and bridal
makeup has made her one of the most sought after makeover specialists
in the country. Renowned for her attention to detail and imaginative eye
makeup, Pooja is a maestro when it comes to bespoke makeovers designed
to suit exceptional needs.

Persistently fuelling her learning curve, Pooja immensely stresses on regular training for herself as well as her expanding team. Having trained with top brands including Illamasqua, NARS, Charlotte Tilbury, Laura Mercier, Urban Decay and YSL in the past, Pooja’s knowledge of premium products and cutting-edge techniques is a superlative quality that reflects in her flawless repute. Along with her creative and confident hairstylist Archana, Pooja has devised countless sensational looks that have earned her unparalleled goodwill.
